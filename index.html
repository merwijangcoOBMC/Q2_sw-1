ENHYPEN'S DISCOROGRAPHY



ENHYPEN, a South Korean boy group formed through the survival show I-LAND and under Belift Lab (a joint venture between Big Hit Entertainment and CJ ENM), debuted on November 30, 2020. Since their debut, ENHYPEN has gained significant attention both domestically and internationally for their captivating music, innovative concepts, and ability to blend various genres. Their discography reflects their growth as artists, from their debut mini-album to their latest releases.



Enhypen's mini albums

BORDER: CARNIVAL

The album blends various musical genres, infusing elements of pop, rock, EDM, and alternative sounds, with a deeper exploration of different moods and textures. The title BORDER: CARNIVAL suggests a world full of extremes—both thrilling and unsettling—as the group navigates the complexities of their journey.

DIMENSION: DILENMA

The concept of DIMENSION: DILEMMA revolves around the paradox of choice and the difficult decisions young people face when confronting the expectations of others versus their own desires. Each track touches on themes of internal conflict, emotional turbulence, and the search for balance in a world that often feels like an overwhelming maze.

ORANGE BLOOD

ORANGE BLOOD symbolizes vibrancy, passion, and the bittersweet emotions of growth. "Blood" represents the group's deep bond with their artistry and fans, reflecting sacrifice and resilience. The album explores themes of strength, rebellion, and the complexities of love and identity as ENHYPEN evolves.

DIMENSION ANSWER

DIMENSION: ANSWER features new tracks alongside songs from DIMENSION: DILEMMA, deepening ENHYPEN’s exploration of self-discovery and the dilemmas they face. The album reflects their growth, with themes of introspection, resolve, and empowerment, while introducing fresh sounds and maintaining their signature emotional depth and genre-blending style.

More of Enhypen's mini albums

BLOSSOM by ENHYPEN is a soft, heartfelt track that symbolizes growth, renewal, and the beauty of love. With gentle melodies and emotional lyrics, it conveys a sense of blossoming into something new and beautiful, reflecting themes of change and hope.

MEMORABILLA

MEMORABILIA by ENHYPEN is a nostalgic, emotional track about cherishing precious memories. With heartfelt lyrics and a soothing melody, it captures the bittersweet beauty of reflecting on meaningful moments.
